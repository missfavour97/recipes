Jollof Rice

Description;

Jollof Rice is one of the most popular and highly regarded dishes in Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • Long-grain parboiled rice (commonly used for the best texture)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Tomato paste (for rich color and flavor)
  • Fresh tomatoes (blended)
  • Red bell peppers
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Onions (blended and sliced)
  • salt
  • curry
  • thyme
  • Bay leaves
  • Seasoning cubes (Maggi or Knorr)
  • Chicken or beef stock (instead of plain water)
  • Butter

Steps;

  1. Blend fresh tomatoes, red bell peppers, Scotch bonnet, and onions into a smooth mix.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry the tomato paste for a few minutes, then add the blended mixture.
  3. Cook until the sauce thickens and the oil begins to separate from the stew.
  4. Add salt, curry powder, thyme, bay leaves, seasoning cubes, and a little black or white pepper.
  5. Stir well and let the sauce simmer for 5/10 minutes to deepen the flavor.
  6. Pour in chicken or beef stock and stir to combine.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessaryâ€”this liquid will determine your rice flavor.
  8. Add washed parboiled rice to the sauce and stir thoroughly to coat the rice evenly.
  9. Cover with foil or a tight lid to trap the steam and cook on low heat until the rice softens.
  10. When the rice is tender and the liquid absorbed, stir gently from bottom to top.
  11. Add a knob of butter for extra richness. Serve with fried plantain, chicken, or salad.
