Lasagna

Description;

Lasagna is a layered Italian pasta dish. It is baked to perfection for a hearty, flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef or minced meat
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning (basil, oregano, thyme)
  • Bay leaf (optional)
  • Ricotta cheese, Mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley
  • Béchamel sauce

Steps;

  1. Heat olive oil, sauté chopped onion and garlic, then add ground beef.
  2. Cook until browned, then stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Boil lasagna sheets in salted water until just tender (or use ovenready sheets)
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese and grated parmesan. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, then add pasta sheets, cheese mixture, and shredded mozzarella.
  6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for about 30 to 40 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake another 10 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
